# Frontend Visual Direction

## Domain

HotelBooking is a hotel booking web app with guest search, room booking, mock payment, customer booking history, and admin operations.

## Primary Users

- Guests comparing available rooms quickly.
- Customers booking and managing stays.
- Admin staff managing rooms, bookings, and users.

## Desired Emotion

The UI should feel calm, high-trust, cinematic, and precise. Users should feel they are booking through a premium travel product, not a classroom CRUD exercise.

## Chosen Direction

Premium cinematic hospitality product with editorial hero imagery, cool glass surfaces, deep slate typography, blue/indigo/cyan accents, restrained ambient glows, and crisp operational admin tables.

## Suitable Styles

- Cinematic landing and room discovery.
- Soft glass search and checkout panels.
- Editorial cards with image depth and subtle hover lift.
- Quiet productivity styling for admin.
- Cool mist background balanced by deep slate, indigo, cyan, blue, and subtle violet highlights.

## Styles To Avoid

- Generic Bootstrap-like cards and buttons.
- Overly playful gradients or saturated purple SaaS themes.
- Heavy 3D scenes that distract from booking.
- Decorative blobs that dominate the interface.
- Marketing-only layout that hides the actual booking workflow.

## Page Treatment

- Home: most premium/cinematic, with strong hero, search card, atmospheric hotel visual, and featured stays.
- Search: product-focused and scannable, with elevated filter bar and room rows.
- Room detail: editorial gallery feel plus sticky conversion panel.
- Auth: premium app-style panel, focused and quiet.
- Booking/payment: confirmation surfaces with strong status hierarchy.
- Admin: clean operational dashboard with dense tables, clear badges, and compact actions.
- Error: simple, polished recovery page.
